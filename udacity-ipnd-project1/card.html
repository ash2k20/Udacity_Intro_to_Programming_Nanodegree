Cat

House cats can hit speeds of around 30 mph which is so fast that they could beat Usain Bolt in a 200-metre dash!

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 46 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 17 years
  • Habitat: Forests, Wetlands, Urban areas

The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. A cat can either be a house cat, a farm cat or a feral cat. Domestic cats are valued by humans for companionship and their ability to hunt rodents.
